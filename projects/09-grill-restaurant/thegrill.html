ABOUT THE GRILL

The Grill is a neighbourhood restaurant built around an open fire and a simple idea: great ingredients, cooked over real flame, shared around a generous table. Our pit masters select cuts every morning and grill them to order so every plate arrives smoky, juicy and full of flavour.

Pull up a chair, order a few small plates to share and let the evening take care of itself.

LATEST AWARDS

We are proud that our work over the grill keeps being noticed. From the city's Best Barbecue title to a place among the top tables for a relaxed night out, recognition only pushes us to keep the fire burning a little brighter.
